Let's Begin!

Kindergarten

Students begin to learn the fundamentals of computer science and design thinking while practicing taking turns, sharing, and honing their observation skills. Together, we explore engineering challenges, we solve problems with code, and we play games that center on math competencies and logic.

Register 

Leveling Up!

First and Second Grade

Students will grow their repertoire of problem solving skills to approach increasing more complex engineering challenges as a group. We will focus on teamwork and co-creation of software while offering many opportunities to develop collaborative skills, practice giving critical feedback, and using flexible thinking.

Register 

Leaping Ahead!

Third and Fourth Grade

Third and Fourth graders will be increasingly challenged with more difficult engineering problems. They will code solutions to real-world problems, explore solvable and unsolvable discrete mathematics challenges, and explore worlds they create in virtual reality.
